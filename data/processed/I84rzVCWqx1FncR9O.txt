Open Education Resourses(OERs)
Open Educational Resources (OER) are freely accessible, openly licensed educational materials that educators, students, and self-learners can use, modify, and redistribute without financial or legal restrictions. These resources provide a flexible and cost-effective way to enhance teaching and learning while promoting the sharing and continuous improvement of educational content.
