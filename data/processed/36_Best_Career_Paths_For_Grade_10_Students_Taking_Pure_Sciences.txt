As learners prepare to transition to Grade 10 in Senior School
following the release of the Kenya Junior School Education
Assessment (KJSEA) results, the Ministry of Education has
outlined possible career paths learners can pursue after
completing Senior School, depending on their chosen pathways.
Under the Competency-Based Education (CBE) curriculum, Pure Sciences form a
specialised track within the STEM pathway. This track is designed for learners
interested in scientific research, innovation, technology, and related professions.
The core subjects in the Pure Sciences track are Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and
Mathematics.
36 Possible Careers for Grade 10 Learners Taking Pure Sciences
At Senior School level, learners select three subjects from their chosen pathway and
combine them with four compulsory core subjects: English, Kiswahili or Kenyan Sign
Language (KSL), Physical Education, and Community Service Learning. These
subject combinations determine the career options learners can pursue after Senior
School.
