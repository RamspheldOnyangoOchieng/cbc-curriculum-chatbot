 PREFACE  
 
It is with great pleasure that I present the List of Approved Rationalised Learning Areas Course Materials for the Year 2024,  developed and validated through a rigorous 
and consult ative process by the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD). This list marks a significant milestone in our ongoing  commitment to provide 
quality, relevant, and inclusive curriculum support materials aligned with the competency -based curriculum ( CBC) framework.  
 
The rationalisation of learning areas is part of a broader national strategy to streamline curriculum content, reduce overloa d, and enhance the depth of learning. In response 
to emerging educational needs and stakeholder feedback, KICD coo rdinated a comprehensive review and evaluation process to ensure that only high -quality, age -
appropriate, and pedagogically sound materials are recommended for use in our schools.  
 
This document serves as a guide to educators, school administrators, publis hers, and other education stakeholders by providing an authoritative list of course materials 
that meet the set standards and have been approved for use in the implementation of the CBC. It reflects KICD’s mandate to en sure that teaching and learning resou rces 
support the holistic development of learners while promoting national values and fostering innovation.  
 
I take this opportunity to appreciate all contributors to this exercise — including subject panel members, curriculum developers, teachers, and pub lishers — for their 
dedication and expertise. As we move forward, KICD remains committed to continuous quality improvement and to supporting the education sector with resources that 
empower learners and teachers alike.  
 
We encourage all stakeholders to use  this list as a reference point in the selection of instructional materials and to stay engaged in the journey of transforming  education 
for a better future.  
 
 
 
 
 
PROF. CHARLES ONG’ONDO, PHD, MBS
